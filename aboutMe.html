Upbringing

I was born in raised in Missouri. My father was a gamer and naturally, I was fascinated with everything that was video games. By the age of 6 years old, I was playing on my father's PlayStation 2 playing all kinds of games.

My family was considered lower middle class since my dad worked a decent construction job.

At 9 years old, my parents separated and my mother got custody of my sister and I.

Education

All the way back in elementary school, I was considered a 'gifted' student. I was quick to learn and especially liked math. Ever since I was little, I always dreamed about building video games as a career.

Middle school and high school had too slow of a learning pace, so I took it apon myself to fulfill the craving to learn within myself.

Starting in 8th grade, I learned how to program in JavaScript. I started making simple applications to learn key concepts. In 12th grade, I was teaching my calculus class concepts that we would learn in the next chapter.

This has stayed the same all through high school and has shown its prevalence into college, as even while I am learning, I am striving to learn even more.
